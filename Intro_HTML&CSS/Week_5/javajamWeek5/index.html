JavaJam Coffee Bar

Follow the Winding Road to JavaJam

We're a little out of the way, but take a drive in the country down Garret Bay Road to JavaJam today! Indulge in out locally roasted free-trade coffee and gome-made pastries. You'll feel right at home at JavaJam!

Friendly and eclectic - JavaJam Coffee Bar is the perfect place to take a break, enjoy a refreshing beverage, and have a snak or light meal.

JavaJam Coffee Bar features:

  • Speciality Coffee and Organic Tea
  • Bagels, Muffins, and Gluten-free Pastries
  • Organic Salads
  • Music and Poetry Readings
  • Open Mic night
